Splatoon 3 Tips and tricks how to use splatana stamper

KEEP ENEMIES AT BAY WITH REPEATED SLASHES.

You can keep enemies, primarily Rollers, Brushes, and Splatana-Type wielding enemies, from pushing up the frontlines. Prevent enemies from taking the rainwater in the Rainmaker game mode and stop them from throwing clams in Clam Blitz by repeatedly swinging your weapon at them!

DESTROYS ENEMY DEPLOYED SUB WEAPONS.

This weapon damages and destroys sub weapons such as the Squid Beakon and Tacticooler much faster than most other weapons. Be sure to use this to your advantage to put pressure on the enemy team and diminish their chances of winning!

DON'T TRY TO PLAY IT AT MELEE/SHORT RANGE.

The melee one hit kill is very situational. I almost exclusively go melee after squid surging a tower someone is occupying or jumping behind someone.

The weapon excels at mid range and keeping pressure. One charged swing and a quick well placed hit with burst bomb is a good kill combo. You can also hit the enemy with a burst bomb first to slow them down and then do a quick charged swing.

The special is of course great for mobility but it can also be used as a situation ink refill.
